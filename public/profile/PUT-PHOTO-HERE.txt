PROFILE PHOTO
=============
Put your photo in THIS folder (public/profile/) named exactly:

  pablo-photo.png   -> shown in the hero glass card

NOTES
- Square works best (recommended ~600x600). It is cropped to a square, never
  stretched. Until you add it, a gradient avatar with your initials "PS" shows.
- PNG, JPG and WebP all work. To use another name/format, update `photo` in
  src/data/profile.ts.
- You can delete this .txt file once your photo is in (optional).
