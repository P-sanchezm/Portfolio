LOGOS
=====
Put institution / team logos in THIS folder (public/logos/) with these names:

  icai-icade.png         -> Universidad Pontificia Comillas ICAI-ICADE
  boston-university.png  -> Boston University
  smu.png                -> Singapore Management University
  icai-rocket-team.png   -> ICAI Rocket Team

(placeholder-logo.svg is already here and used for LinkedIn-imported entries.)

NOTES
- Square logos with a TRANSPARENT background look best (recommended ~256x256).
- Logos are fitted INSIDE the badge (object-contain) - the whole logo shows,
  it is NOT cropped. Any size works.
- Until you add one, a monogram of the organization's initials is shown.
- File names must match what's in src/data/timeline.ts (the `logo` field).
- You can delete this .txt file once your logos are in (optional).
